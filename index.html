The Foundation

Rising from the Roots

Grinding since February 14, 2026

I began my journey in Computer Science (BSc. CSIT) in 2024 with a mindset fixed on the long game. On February 13, 2026, a simple thought: if I record my day, maybe I will actually do something with it(I just wanted to be productive). The next morning, February 14, I started posting on YouTube. Not because I had something big to show. Just to keep myself moving. I wanted to share the process, the struggle, the growth. I wanted to be honest about it. And that is how The Night Journey started. It is not a polished story. It is not a highlight reel. It is just me, trying to figure things out, day by day, and sharing it with others.

The Craft

Creating with Intent

Right now I am learning full-stack web development. But honestly, the bigger thing I am focused on is self improvement. Getting better as a person, as a thinker, as someone who follows through. The technical skills are part of that. I am trying to build good habits around how I learn, how I work, and how I carry myself. I am taking it step by step and not rushing. I know what I want to become. I just have to keep on going, keep on learning. That is the craft for me.

The Vision

Evolution & Intelligence

I am studying computer science right now and my real interest is in AI and Data Science. That is where I want to go after graduation. I have a rough picture in my head of what I want to do with my life and the kind of person I want to become. It is bigger than just a job or a title. I am not ready to say all of it yet. But I know the map. And I am moving toward it, slowly and seriously.

Capabilities

Skills & Strengths

I think a lot. Maybe too much sometimes. But over time I have started to understand how I think, what patterns I fall into, and where I tend to get stuck. I am trying to use that self awareness to actually improve instead of just thinking about improving. I am not the most disciplined person. I know that. But I am honest about it and I am working on it. I notice things quickly, I am not afraid to speak, and I care about doing the right thing. I still have a lot to prove, mostly to myself.

Beliefs

Faith & Respect

I believe in God. I am rooted in my Hindu faith. But I also thing we should believe strongly in ourself, our hard work, and the people around us. Faith without effort is just waiting. I think we have to show up for our own life. I respect every path people walk as long as they are genuine about it. I do not like beliefs that make people feel better than others from religious perspectives. My goal is simple: be humble, be honest, and treat all the people well. That is enough for me. I want to remember that when I am successful, when I have something to show, and when I have a platform. I want to use it to do good, to help others, and to be a positive influence in the world. That is the philosophy I am trying to live by.
